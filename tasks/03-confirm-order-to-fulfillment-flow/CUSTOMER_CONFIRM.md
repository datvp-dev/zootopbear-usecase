# Cau hoi confirm voi khach: Luong tao don den khi co tracking

## Muc tieu buoi confirm

Can lam ro mot luong quan trong nhat:

Seller dua don vao Zootop Bear, he thong tinh tien, tru tien, gui don sang xuong/nen tang san xuat, sau do cap nhat trang thai va tracking cho seller xem.

Khach khong can tra loi theo goc nhin ky thuat. Chi can xac nhan cach cong ty muon van hanh thuc te.

## Cau hoi so 1 can chot truoc

Sau khi seller tao/import don va du tien trong vi, he thong nen xu ly nhu the nao?

- Cach 1: Tu dong gui thang sang xuong/nen tang san xuat.
- Cach 2: Don vao danh sach cho noi bo kiem tra, sau do nhan vien moi bam gui sang xuong.

Neu khach chi chon duoc mot cau trong buoi dau tien, nen chot cau nay truoc.

## Nhom 1: Seller dua don vao he thong

1. Seller se tao don bang cach nao trong giai doan dau?
   - Nhap tung don tren web.
   - Upload file Excel/CSV.
   - Ket noi API tu he thong rieng cua seller.

2. Khi upload file co 100 don, neu 5 don bi sai thong tin thi muon xu ly the nao?
   - Van nhan 95 don dung, 5 don sai bao loi rieng.
   - Khong nhan ca file, bat seller sua roi upload lai.

3. Mot don co nhieu san pham thi seller dang quen nhap nhu the nao?
   - Moi san pham mot dong, cung ma don.
   - Tat ca san pham nam tren mot dong.

4. Link thiet ke/mockup seller gui len co can he thong kiem tra mo duoc khong?
   - Co, neu link khong mo duoc thi bao loi ngay.
   - Khong, chi luu link; khi xuong/van hanh loi thi xu ly sau.

## Nhom 2: Tinh tien cho seller

1. Seller can thay tong tien va chi tiet tien truoc khi bam xac nhan don khong?

2. Voi don ship bang label/TikTok, cong thuc hien tai co dung khong?

```text
Tien don = (gia san pham + phi label) x so luong
```

3. Voi don ship by seller mot san pham, cong thuc hien tai co dung khong?

```text
Tien don = gia san pham x so luong + phi ship
```

4. Voi don nhieu san pham, khach cho xin 2-3 vi du tinh tien thuc te:
   - San pham nao tinh phi ship chinh?
   - San pham nao tinh phi them item?
   - Neu moi san pham co phi ship khac nhau thi lay phi nao?

## Nhom 3: Tru tien, thieu tien, credit

1. Seller bam xac nhan don thi he thong tru tien ngay hay chi tam giu tien?

2. Neu seller khong du tien, don nen o dau?
   - Luu lai trang thai cho nap tien.
   - Khong tao don.
   - Dua vao danh sach cho noi bo xem.

3. Don thieu tien co can nhan vien noi bo thay khong, hay chi seller thay?

4. Co seller nao duoc cho len don truoc khi nap du tien khong?
   - Khong.
   - Co, nhung chi seller dac biet.

5. Neu co seller dac biet, can chot:
   - Ai duoc phep cho no tien.
   - No toi da bao nhieu.
   - No trong bao lau.
   - Co bat buoc ghi ly do khong.

## Nhom 4: Gui don sang xuong/nen tang san xuat

1. Khi nao don duoc gui sang xuong?
   - Ngay sau khi seller xac nhan va du tien.
   - Sau khi nhan vien noi bo kiem tra.
   - Gui theo dot trong ngay.

2. Mot san pham co the co nhieu xuong lam duoc thi chon xuong theo tieu chi nao?
   - Theo san pham da cau hinh san.
   - Theo gia tot hon.
   - Theo xuong uu tien.
   - Theo tinh trang xuong/API dang hoat dong.

3. Neu gui don sang xuong bi loi thi muon xu ly the nao?
   - He thong tu gui lai.
   - Chuyen don sang trang thai can xu ly.
   - Nhan vien tu thao tac lai.
   - Chuyen sang xuong khac neu co.

4. Trong 5 nen tang hien tai, nen tang nao da chac chan co the gui don tu dong trong phase 1?
   - FlashShip.
   - SimplePrint.
   - PrintBelle.
   - ART ADD.
   - Snap Ecom.

5. Nen tang nao chua gui tu dong duoc thi doi noi bo se lam the nao?
   - Xuat file roi upload len nen tang do.
   - Nhan vien nhap tay tren portal cua nen tang.
   - Tam thoi chua dua vao go-live.

## Nhom 5: Trang thai don va tracking

1. Seller can thay nhung trang thai nao?
   - Moi tao/import.
   - Cho thanh toan.
   - Da xac nhan.
   - Dang san xuat.
   - Da co tracking.
   - Dang van chuyen.
   - Da giao.
   - Dang loi/can xu ly.
   - Da huy.
   - Da hoan tien.

2. Tracking lay tu dau la chinh?
   - Tu xuong/nen tang san xuat.
   - Tu 17TRACK.
   - Ca hai.

3. Neu xuong bao mot trang thai, 17TRACK bao trang thai khac, thi uu tien ben nao?

4. Seller can thay thong tin tracking nao?
   - Ma tracking.
   - Don vi van chuyen.
   - Link tracking.
   - Trang thai van chuyen.
   - Ngay giao du kien.

5. Neu don da ship nhung chua co tracking sau bao lau thi can bao cho nhan vien noi bo xu ly?

## Ket qua can co sau khi khach tra loi

Sau khi khach tra loi, se chot duoc:

1. Don di tu dong hay can nhan vien duyet truoc.
2. File loi thi nhan mot phan hay bat sua ca file.
3. Cong thuc tinh tien chuan.
4. Cach xu ly thieu tien, credit dac biet va refund.
5. Khi nao gui don sang xuong.
6. Neu gui loi thi ai xu ly va xu ly the nao.
7. Seller se thay nhung trang thai va tracking nao.
